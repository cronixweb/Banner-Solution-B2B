About Us

Not Just a Hardware Distributor

Banner Solutions is more than a typical door hardware distributor – we’re obsessed with making our customers’ jobs easier through our inventory availability, product findability, and top-tier customer support. Our flagship distribution center in Texas ensures rapid shipping, with over 98% of orders shipped the same day, and backed by advanced technology for exceptional order accuracy. Our expertise spans commercial, electronic access control, residential hardware, and locksmith supplies, supported by an extensive e-commerce platform featuring products from over 260 manufacturers. Banner Solutions aims to simplify and enhance customer experiences through efficient processes and personalized service, setting new standards in the industry. We’re focused on turning your challenges into opportunities, so you can deliver extraordinary results.

  • What we do

    Banner Solutions stocks a wide range of security hardware products for Commercial, Residential, Access Control, and Automotive applications. We make it easy for you to find and order those products. And then, we deliver them fast. We are your resource for fill-in orders, shortages, replacements, upgrades, and anything you need quickly. Beyond covering your day-to-day needs, our group of experts are ready to partner with you to solve complex security projects for your customers. Banner Solutions has a history of partnership with the best manufacturers throughout the industry, making it easy to quote and deliver for every kind of projects.

  • How we deliver

    We simplify the complex. This may mean consolidating your purchases into one order with one shipment, or helping you design and source the right mix of products to complete a project on time and in budget. Whatever the scenario, we are a team with deep, industry specific knowledge working together, applying our combined strengths and expertise to support your business.

  • Where we are going

    In a time where the lines have become blurry, know that we work alongside you and will not compete against you. We are your partners and will continue to be an industry leader with a DNA of continuous improvement. Making your job easier and solving the previously unsolvable is our measure of success.
